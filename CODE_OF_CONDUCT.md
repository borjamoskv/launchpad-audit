# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Keep feedback specific, technical, and actionable.
- Assume contributors have different contexts and constraints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Derailing issues or pull requests with unrelated arguments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants when needed to keep the project useful and safe for contributors.
